## Production Time Estimates

Assuming 6 labs (2 input, 4 output), no cooldowns:

| Compound | Tier | Reactions | Time Estimate |
|----------|------|-----------|---------------|
| OH | 1 | 1 step | ~10 ticks/1000 units |
| UH | 2 | 1 step | ~10 ticks/1000 units |
| UH2O | 3 | 2 steps | ~20 ticks/1000 units |
| XUH2O | 4 | 3 steps | ~30 ticks/1000 units |

**Note:** Each lab has 10-tick cooldown, so actual production is ~4 units per tick per output lab.

With 4 output labs: ~16 units/tick = ~960 units/minute
